3Ô∏è‚É£ Heat Sinks + Thermal Paste (CPU cooling core)
CPU Heat Sink and Fan Assembly
Heat sinks

Two types:

  • Passive heat sink (no fan)
  • Active heat sink (heat sink + fan = common CPU cooler)

Materials

  • Copper or aluminum (with fins)
Thermal paste / thermal pad

Placed between:

  • CPU
  • Heatsink

Why? Because tiny air gaps block heat transfer.

Thermal pad

  • Easier to apply
  • Softens with heat
  • Can be less reliable than paste
üß† Exam rule:
Paste/pad exists to remove air gaps.
